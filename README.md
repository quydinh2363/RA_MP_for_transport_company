## :large_blue_diamond: Risk Assessment Process
| PROCESS               | DESCRIPTION                                                                 |
|-----------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Risk Identif  | Identifying potential risks through various methods such as brainstorming, expert interviews, and analysis of past incidents. |
| Risk Analysis         | Identified risks based on their impact and likelihood.        |
| Risk Evaluation       | Prioritizing the risks to determine which ones require immediate attention and mitigation. |
